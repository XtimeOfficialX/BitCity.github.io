What is BITCITY

The meta universe world based on Binance Smart Chain. A new experience meta-universe scene launched by XTime. To obtain account login permission through XTime's NFT, each NFT is regarded as a login account. You can build your own world in BITCITY. You can build schools, airports, shops, houses, etc. The game uses $XTime and $Bitcoin to pay for game props and purchase land.
